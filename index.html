Ankith Jain Rakesh Kumar

I am a fourth-year Ph.D student majoring in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California Riverside.

I work under the supervision of Dr. Bir Bhanu at Visualization and Intelligent Systems Laboratory (VisLab). My research interests are Computer Vision and Deep Learning, especially in video analysis research (classification, detection, and segmentation), facial micro-expressions, graph networks and applications, and recommendation systems. I got my M.S. from the University of California Riverside and B.E. from Visvesvaraya Technological University.
