Ankith Jain Rakesh Kumar

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Riverside.

I work under the supervision of Dr. Bir Bhanu in the Visualization and Intelligent Systems Laboratory (VisLab). My research focuses on Computer Vision, Graph Neural Networks, Deep Learning, and Biomedical Imaging. I specialize in graph-based architectures for facial micro-expression analysis, molecular data modeling, and gaze-informed medical image classification.

I hold an M.S. in Electrical Engineering from UCR and a B.E. from Visvesvaraya Technological University.
